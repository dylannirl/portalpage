General Reflection

The most challegning part was definitly the communication and being able to align ideas with everybody on the team. The part I enjoyed most was being able to create with skills that we learned. Something I would do differently if I could do this project again is make sure to get phone numbers of my clients, developers, etc to allow for better communication. I do feel confident in working on real world projects because I now know the skills, other than just html and css, that it takes to actually develop for a project. Communication and design are important and I learned that here.
